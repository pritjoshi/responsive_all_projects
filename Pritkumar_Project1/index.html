Veg Cheese Sandwich

Description

This veg Cheese Sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right. It is healthy and tastes yummy as well.

Ingridents

  • 2 Slices of white bread or multi-grain bread or brown bread
  • Green Chutney
  • Tomato Ketchup
  • Peanut Butter Mayonnaise
  • Cucumber
  • Boiled Potato - cutted into rounds
  • Tomato - cutted into rounds
  • Boiled Beat - cutted into rounds
  • Chat Masala
  • Cheese
  • Sharp Cutting Knife
  • Cutting Board
  • Butter Knife

Directions

  1. Lay both sides of bread next to each other on a cutting board.
  2. With the butter knife, spread 1/8th layer of bread with equal amount of green chutney, tomato ketchup and peanut butter mayonnaise on the bread.
  3. Place bread slices together.
  4. Use the sharp knife to cut the sandwich diagonally and remove the crust on the cutting board.
  5. Put cucumber on bread and spread a pinch of chat masala on cucumber.
  6. Put round cutted potatoes. Again add a pinch of chat masala.
  7. Put round cutted tomatoes and add a pinch of chat masala.
  8. Lastly, put round cutted boiled beat and add a pinch of chat masala.
  9. Now, take another piece of bread, press the bread lightly.
  10. Cut the sandwich with sharp knife with the shape of pieces as you want.
  11. Grate lots of cheese on sandwich and sprinkle some chat masala on it to taste good.

Your Veg Cheese Sandwich is ready to hit the hunger.
Enjoy your Sandwich...
